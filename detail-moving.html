Stanley Park

Vancouver's first, largest, and most beloved urban park! Stanley Park is a magnificent green oasis in the midst of the urban landscape of Vancouver.

Location Vancouver, BC V6G 1Z4
Time to spend Half to a full day
Activities
  • Join a guided tour and see Stanley Park by horse-drawn carriage, shuttle trolley, or tour bus
  • Go along the 28 km Stanley Park Seawall on foot, bike or skate
  • Explore the park's flower gardens, landmarks, monuments and sculptures
  • Learn about aquatic lives in Vancouver Aquarium
Operation time From 9:00am to 5:00pm
Addmission fee Free except for Vancouver Aquarium
Elderly, Disability, Children and Pets friendly Yes

Deer Lake Park

Situated in a peaceful natural environment that serves as the cultural precinct for the City of Burnaby's arts and heritage amenities.

Location 6450 Deer Lake Dr, Burnaby, BC V5E 2L8
Time to spend Half to a full day
Activities
  • Hire pedalos, row boats or kayaks and enjoy the lake
  • Walk the 5 km trail around the lake. And try to spot some wildlives along the way
  • Go back to the 1920s and learn history with Burnaby Village Museum
Operation time From 6:00am to 10:00pm
Addmission fee Free
Elderly, Disability, Children and Pets friendly Yes

Capilano Suspension Bridge

Capilano Suspension Bridge crosses to towering evergreens, cedar-scented rainforest air and Treetops Adventure, 7 suspended footbridges offering views 110 feet above the forest floor.

Location 3735 Capilano Rd, North Vancouver, BC V7R 4J1
Time to spend Half a day
Activities
  • Enjoy the beautiful nature around Capilano River while walking across the Suspension Bridge, the Cliff Walk or the Treetops Adventure
  • Visit the Story Center to learn about the history of the Bridge and of Vancouver
  • Celebrate Winter with the Canyon Lights Festival. The park will be transformed into a world of festive lights and visual enchantment
Operation time From 8:00am to 8:00pm
Addmission fee From $14.95 to $46.95 (Free for children under 6)
Elderly, Disability, Children and Pets friendly No for individuals with mobility difficulty.
