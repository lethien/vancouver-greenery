Burnaby Mountain

Located within the Burnaby Mountain Conservation Area, Burnaby Mountain Hiking and Biking Trails consists of a network of 26 multi-use trails covering 28 kilometres criss-crosses the 576 hectares.

Location University Dr E, Burnaby, BC V5A 4Y8
Time to spend Half to a full day
Activities
  • Set off on a vigorous hike, a leisurely stroll or a heart-pumping bike ride
  • Visit the Centennial Rose Garden on top of the mountain
  • Warning: Black bears and cougars are occasional spotted in the trails
Operation time From 8:00am to 8:00pm
Addmission fee Free
Elderly, Disability, Children and Pets friendly No for individuals with mobility difficulty

Lynn Canyon Park

Opened to the public in 1912, Lynn Canyon Park has been a popular destination among tourists and local residents of Lynn Valley ever since. Featuring a suspension bridge that sways 50 meters above the canyon, also a selection of walking and hiking trails.

Location 3690 Park Rd, North Vancouver, BC V7J 3K2
Time to spend Half to a full day
Activities
  • Enjoy the bridges, waterfalls and swimming holes within the park
  • Learn about natural history as well as local and global environmental issues at Lynn Canyon Ecology Centre
  • Conquer beautiful hiking trails that are suitable for any hiking level, whatever if you are looking for a leisurely stroll through the forest or a brisk climb up hill
Operation time From 10:00am to 5:00pm
Addmission fee Free
Elderly, Disability, Children and Pets friendly No for individuals with mobility difficulty

Grouse Mountain

During the summer, guests enjoy hiking, Zip Line Adventures, paragliding, sightseeing, lumberjack shows and more. In winter, we have skiing, snowboarding, snowshoeing, sleigh rides among many other activities.

Location 6400 Nancy Greene Way, North Vancouver, BC V7R 4K9
Time to spend Half to a full day
Activities
  • Experience breathtaking views of the city of Vancouver, sparkling Pacific Ocean, Gulf Islands, and snowy peaks unfold as you journey up the mountainside on a Sky Ride or Chairlift Ride
  • Fly across the peaks and canyons of Grouse and Dam Mountains with an adrenaline-pumping Mountain Ziplines tour
  • Conquer the “Mother Nature’s Stairmaster” as you complete the 2.9-kilometre trail, The Grouse Grind, up the face of Grouse Mountain
Operation time From 9:00am to 10:00pm
Addmission fee From $29 to $56 (Free for children under 4)
Elderly, Disability, Children and Pets friendly No for individuals with mobility difficulty.
