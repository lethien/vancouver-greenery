VanDusen Garden

VanDusen Botanical Garden is a 55-acre oasis in the heart of Vancouver with over 7,500 plant species and varieties from around the world! VanDusen has something for everyone to enjoy!

Location 5251 Oak St, Vancouver, BC V6M 4H1
Time to spend Half to a full day
Activities
  • Explore Visitor Centre Featuring advanced technology, a green roof, and beautiful orchid-inspired design, the building is targeting LEED Platinum status
  • Enjoy Gardens which contain hundred of seasonal flowers, plants, wildlives and arts
  • Discover Landscapes such as The Elizabethan Maze, Stone Garden, Scottish Shelter and Korean Pavilion
Operation time From 9:00am to 8:00pm (May differ by season)
Addmission fee From $4.25 to $11.25 (Free for children under 3)
Elderly, Disability, Children and Pets friendly Yes

Queen Elizabeth Park

Vancouver’s horticultural jewel, is a major draw for floral display enthusiasts and view-seekers, and as a popular backdrop for wedding photos. It’s the highest point in Vancouver and makes for spectacular views of the park, city, and mountains on the North Shore.

Location 4600 Cambie St, Vancouver, BC V5Z 2Z1
Time to spend Half to a full day
Activities
  • Enjoy Gardens with seasonal flowers: Quarry Gardens, Arboretum, Rose Garden
  • Experience the colours and scents of the tropics year-round in the Bloedel Conservatory
  • Relax next to The fabulous Dancing Waters fountain both day and night. It is comprised of 70 jets of water using 85,000 litres of recirculating water
Operation time From 6:00am to 10:00pm
Addmission fee Free except for the Bloedel Conservatory
Elderly, Disability, Children and Pets friendly Yes for children and pets. No for individuals with mobility difficulty.
