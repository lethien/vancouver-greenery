Get Relaxed

Spend your leisure time with the nature in Vancouver's hundreds of parks.
Or walk through our beautiful flower gardens.

Local Parks

With more than 200 parks, it's likely that you will find one just outside your door.
Sun-bathing, walking, sport-playing, ... anything you would like to do.

VanDusen Garden

The multi-award winning LEED Platinum Visitor Centre.
A 55-acre oasis in the heart of Vancouver with over 7,500 plant species and varieties from around the world.

Queen Elizabeth Park

One of the most delightful walking areas in the City.
A beautiful city park with flower gardens, public art, grassy knolls, and panoramic views over the city.

Explore the 400-hectare natural rainforest, water, mountains, sky, majestic trees and the famous Seawall.

Get Challenged

Vancouver's mountains have some of the most beautiful hiking routes.
Look for more? Some routes allow mountain biking as well.

Burnaby Mountain

Set off on a vigorous hike, a leisurely stroll or a heart-pumping bike ride.
A network of 26 multi-use trails covering 28 kilometres criss-crosses the 576 hectares.

Lynn Canyon Park

Several trails throughout the park to choose from for a hike.
There are also many breathtaking waterfalls and popular swimming holes that are perfect for a refreshing swim.

Grouse Mountain

A 1,200-metre mountain offering activities such as skiing and hiking, plus sweeping views of Vancouver.
